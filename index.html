Introduction

Nucleus Technology is the Start-up company that is developing safety and minimalist mini-nuclear reactors for civilian and military use to provide necessary electricity and power.

Currently we have successfully developed the MR-01A Prototype Portable mini-reactor and the CR-01CD Portable mini-reactors. Both mini-reactors can continue nuclear reactions for 10 years and after 10 year nuclear reactors must refill their nuclear fuels.

Our Products

MR-01A Prototype Portable Mini-Nuclear Reactor

  • Dedicated to provide electricity to necessary military use such as vehicles, buildings and other uses
  • Can provide 300 kilowatt hour power
  • 15 years of use, then must refill the fuel
  • 300m2 area required and 30 kilogram
  • This model haven't in mass production yet

CR-01CD Portable Mini-Nuclear Reactor

  • Dedicated to provide electricity to necessary civilian use such as vehicles and buildings and other uses
  • Can provide 235 kilowatt hour power
  • 15 years of use, then must refill the fuel
  • 215m2 area required and 25 kilogram
  • You can buy from Walmart
Manual of Products

CR-01CD Portable Mini-Reactor

  1. Place it in comfortable place or building
  2. Tap (Start) button
  3. Control must give (Waiting) message
  4. Then a few moments later control must give (Reaction on going) message
  5. Then after reaction start control said (Ready to transfer)
  6. And you can connect your power cables into reactor and control said (Connected)
